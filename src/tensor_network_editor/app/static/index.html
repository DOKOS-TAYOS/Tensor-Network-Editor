How to use the editor

A quick guide for navigation, templates, engines, selection, notes, the Contract planner, exports, and shortcuts.

Navigation

  • Use Ctrl/Cmd + wheel or a pinch gesture to zoom.
  • Use the mouse wheel or trackpad without Ctrl/Cmd to pan the canvas.
  • Drag empty canvas space to move the view.
  • Use the minimap to recenter or drag the visible area.

Selection

  • Left click selects one tensor, index, or connection.
  • Drag with right click to select many elements at once. Box selection also includes notes.
  • Use Ctrl/Cmd+C and Ctrl/Cmd+V to copy and paste a tensor subgraph.

Organize and edit

  • Templates insert ready-to-edit network layouts with the expected open legs and bond structure.
  • Use Graph size, Bond dimension, and Physical dimension before inserting a template.
  • Graph size means sites for MPS and MPO, side length for PEPS, and depth for MERA or Binary Tree.
  • Choose the code engine that fits your workflow, including NumPy einsum and PyTorch einsum.
  • Drag one selected tensor to move all selected tensors together.
  • Use the multi-selection panel to recolor or delete everything selected.
  • If only tensors are selected, you can add one default index to each tensor.
  • Resize tensors from the corner handles directly on the canvas.
  • Use Group to keep related tensors together and drag the group box as one block.
  • Notes can be moved, resized, collapsed, and expanded directly on the canvas, and they move together with other selected items.
  • Notes, groups, colors, and copied selections are all part of the saved design.

Connect and contract

  • Activate Connect and click two open indices with the same dimension.
  • Activate Contract in the planner, then click tensors or previous steps to build a manual contraction path.
  • Auto future plans the remaining visible operands, while Auto past replans the tensors that are already inside contracted results.
  • The preview buttons are toggles: pressing the same one again disables it, and opening another preview or a manual step closes the previous preview.
  • Export PNG for a clean image or SVG for a vector version.
  • Undo and redo affect the design, not the current zoom or viewport.
